Delivered the way
your team learns best.

All training programs are available in three delivery formats — choose the model that fits your team's size, location and scheduling constraints, or combine formats across different role groups.

Instructor-Led — In Person

On-site delivery at your location using your own ServiceNow environment. Hands-on sessions with direct facilitator interaction and real-time Q&A. Best for teams that benefit from focused group learning and collaborative exercises.

Best for: cohort training · team kick-offs · post-implementation enablement

Instructor-Led — Virtual

Live virtual delivery via Microsoft Teams or Zoom. Structured sessions with hands-on exercises in your environment. Suitable for distributed teams, interstate participants or where travel is not practical.

Best for: distributed teams · flexible scheduling · regional organisations

Custom eLearning

Bespoke eLearning modules recorded against your specific platform configuration — for self-paced completion and ongoing onboarding of new staff who join after the initial training delivery.

Best for: self-paced learning · new staff onboarding · ongoing enablement
