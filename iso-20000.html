ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018

Introducing ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 – IT Service Management System

Globally recognized best practice, ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 provides the structured framework and flexibility organizations need to plan, establish, implement, operate, monitor, review, maintain, and continually improve an IT service management system (SMS). It helps organizations deliver IT services of consistent quality, building customer confidence and operational resilience while supporting alignment with broader business objectives.

The benefits of certification

Independent certification demonstrates your organization’s commitment to excellence in IT service delivery and management.

By gaining third-party assurance that your IT service management system meets the requirements of ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018, you can inspire confidence in your ability to deliver reliable, high-quality IT services, manage service-related risks, and build trust with clients and stakeholders through an internationally recognized mark of service management excellence.

Why organizations require ISO 20000-1:2018?

Organizations adopt ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 to establish a structured and internationally recognized approach for managing IT services consistently and effectively. In today’s technology-dependent environment, organizations rely on IT services to support core business processes, customer interactions, and operational continuity. ISO 20000-1 provides a systematic framework to govern service delivery while supporting business objectives and demonstrating accountability to customers and regulators.

Quality & Risk Management

A primary reason for implementing ISO 20000-1 is IT service quality and risk management. The standard enables organizations to define service requirements clearly, establish governance and accountability structures for service delivery, manage incidents, problems, changes, and releases in a controlled manner, and continually measure and improve service performance. Rather than responding reactively to service failures, organizations build proactive operational resilience.

Regulatory & Compliance

ISO 20000-1 also supports regulatory, contractual, and procurement compliance. Many industries and government bodies require IT service providers to demonstrate structured service management capabilities as a condition of contract award or regulatory authorization. Certification provides independently verified evidence that service management practices are properly designed, implemented, and continuously monitored, helping organizations meet client obligations and tender requirements.

Trust & Advantage

A key driver is trust and competitive advantage. Certification reassures clients, partners, and oversight bodies that IT services are managed responsibly, reliably, and in accordance with internationally accepted best practice. Increasingly, clients require ISO 20000-1 certification as a prerequisite for IT outsourcing and managed service engagements, making it a strategic enabler for market access and long-term client relationships.

Organizational Governance

The standard strengthens organizational governance by defining clear roles, responsibilities, policies, and performance measurement processes across all aspects of the IT service management system. Service management becomes a structured organizational capability rather than an ad hoc operational activity, with board-level visibility through defined service reporting and continual improvement processes.

Continual Improvement

Finally, ISO 20000-1 promotes a culture of continual improvement through regular audits, management reviews, and corrective actions. This ensures service management practices evolve alongside changing business requirements, new service technologies, and evolving client expectations.

Unlock Opportunities
