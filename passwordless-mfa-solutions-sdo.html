Phishing-Resistant Authentication Across Enterprise Identities

Clarent360 implements passwordless and multi-factor authentication solutions that eliminate credential-based attack vectors without degrading user experience. Passwords remain the leading cause of identity compromise — phishing, credential stuffing, and password reuse collectively account for the majority of enterprise breaches.

Our passwordless MFA delivery model replaces legacy authentication with FIDO2, Windows Hello, certificate-based, and biometric controls — integrated with Microsoft Entra ID, Active Directory Federation Services, and third-party identity providers.

INTERACTIVE FRAMEWORK

Pillars of a Robust Passwordless MFA Programme

A successful passwordless programme requires more than deploying authenticators. Each pillar addresses a distinct phase of the transition — from readiness assessment to full deployment and ongoing management.

Assess & Inventory

Catalogue all authentication methods, identity providers, and application dependencies to establish a complete passwordless migration baseline.

Design & Select

Choose appropriate passwordless methods for each user population and application — aligning technology choices to risk level and usability requirements.

Deploy & Enrol

Roll out authenticators, enrolment workflows, and helpdesk processes — managing the transition with phased deployment and fallback controls.

Test & Validate

Validate authentication flows, edge cases, and fallback scenarios across all platforms before broad deployment.

Enable & Train

Prepare end users and helpdesk teams with clear guidance, self-service enrolment tools, and support escalation paths.

Monitor & Manage

Track authentication health, failed enrolments, and policy exceptions — refining controls as the programme matures and coverage expands.
