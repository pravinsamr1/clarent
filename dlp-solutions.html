Pillars of a Robust DLP Solution

Effective data loss prevention is not a single control — it is a layered architecture. Each pillar below addresses a distinct risk surface, operating together to close the gaps that point solutions leave open.

Discover & Classify

Locate sensitive data across all environments and apply policy-driven sensitivity labels before risk accumulates.

Monitor & Detect

Continuous inspection of data flows across email, endpoints, and cloud channels to identify policy violations in real time.

Enforce & Protect

Automated policy actions — block, encrypt, quarantine — applied at the point of violation without manual intervention.

Restrict & Rights-Manage

Persistent DRM binds access controls to the file itself — enforced whether the document is inside or outside your perimeter.

Report & Evidence

Audit-ready dashboards and incident logs that demonstrate compliance posture to regulators and internal stakeholders.

Tune & Mature

Ongoing policy refinement based on incident patterns, false positive rates, and evolving data classification requirements.
