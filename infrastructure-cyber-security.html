SERVICE OVERVIEW

End-to-end security engineering and infrastructure services built for today's threat landscape.

Clarent360 delivers practical cybersecurity and infrastructure solutions that help organisations reduce exposure, strengthen compliance and maintain operational resilience. From Zero Trust architecture and cloud security reviews to penetration testing and GRC audits, our engagements are mapped directly to your risk profile, platform stack and regulatory obligations.

Whether you are securing a Microsoft 365 environment, hardening virtualised infrastructure, managing DLP policies, or preparing for an Essential Eight or ISO 27001 audit, our team brings the engineering depth and advisory rigour to get it right.
