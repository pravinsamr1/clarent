Data Privacy Program

Bolster your privacy posture with Clarent360’s Data Privacy Program

The Data Privacy Program by Clarent360 is an enterprise data protection and privacy framework designed to help organizations build resilient, compliant, and secure digital environments with privacy as a priority.

Our DPP focuses into several functional domains, not limited to:

  • System development

  • Digital transformation initiatives

  • Cloud adoption

  • Vendor onboarding

  • Business process design

Our privacy specialists work closely with executive leadership, technology teams, and business units to ensure accountability and sustainable privacy governance.

Privacy Incident Response & Data Breach Management

  • Evaluation of data breach events for privacy impact
  • Incident classification and regulatory reporting support
  • Breach containment and mitigation advisory
  • Post-incident privacy risk assessment
  • Lessons-learned and control improvement programs

Privacy Governance & Compliance

Clarent360 establishes strong privacy governance structures aligned with international best practices.

  • Identification of applicable privacy laws and regulatory obligations
  • Development of privacy policies, standards, and control frameworks
  • Governance of organizational privacy controls
  • Assignment of data protection responsibilities
  • Oversight and assurance of privacy control implementation
  • Privacy consulting for business and technology teams
  • Validation and testing of privacy safeguards prior to deployment

Data Protection Officer (DPO) Services

Clarent360 provides expert Data Protection Officer (DPO) support to ensure compliant handling of personal data throughout its lifecycle.

  • Collaboration with business process owners on data protection requirements

  • Performance of Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs)

  • Continuous evaluation of data handling practices

  • Monitoring regulatory compliance obligations

  • Privacy awareness and secure data handling training

  • Advisory services for cross-border data transfers and regulatory alignment
