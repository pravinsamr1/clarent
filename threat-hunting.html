INTERACTIVE FRAMEWORK

Pillars of a Robust Threat Hunting Operation

Effective threat hunting is not reactive — it is a structured, repeatable methodology. Each pillar addresses a distinct phase of adversary activity, working together to compress dwell time and eliminate persistent threats.

Hunt & Hypothesise

Form threat hypotheses based on intelligence feeds, TTPs, and environmental baselines before scanning for evidence.

Collect & Correlate

Aggregate telemetry from endpoints, network logs, and cloud audit trails into a unified investigation surface.

Detect & Investigate

Identify anomalous patterns, trace lateral movement, and attribute activity to known or novel threat actors.

Contain & Neutralise

Execute isolation, revocation, and remediation actions to remove the threat before further damage occurs.

Document & Tune

Record findings, update detection rules, and refine configurations based on each engagement.

Report & Brief

Deliver executive and technical reports with root cause analysis, timeline reconstruction, and remediation guidance.
