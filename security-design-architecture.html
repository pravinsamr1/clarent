Security architecture is most effective when it follows a disciplined engagement model. Each pillar ensures that designs are grounded in evidence, operationally deliverable, and aligned to the organisation's risk appetite.

Understand the existing environment — platforms, controls, data flows, and threat landscape — before any design work begins.
