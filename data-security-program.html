Digital Security Program

Strengthen Your Cybersecurity Posture with Clarent360’s Digital Security Program

The Digital Security Program by Clarent360 is an enterprise cybersecurity and data protection framework designed to help organizations build resilient, compliant, and secure digital environments.

As cyber threats continue to evolve, organizations require more than isolated security tools. They need a structured cybersecurity program that integrates governance, risk management, compliance, and operational security into everyday business processes.

Clarent360’s Digital Security Program enables medium and large organizations to establish a risk-driven Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned with global standards, privacy regulations, and modern cybersecurity best practices.

Why Your Organization Needs a Digital Security Program

  • Data breaches and ransomware attacks
  • Operational disruption and downtime
  • Regulatory penalties and compliance failures
  • Financial and reputational damage
  • Unauthorized access to sensitive information

Core Security Principles of Clarent360’s DSP

Confidentiality

Protect sensitive data from unauthorized access and disclosure while safeguarding personal privacy and proprietary business information.

Integrity

Ensure data accuracy, authenticity, and protection against unauthorized modification or destruction.

Availability

Maintain reliable and timely access to systems, applications, and digital services essential for business operations.

Safety

Reduce risks associated with technology failure, cyber manipulation, or malicious activities that could impact operational or physical safety.

Key Objectives of the Digital Security Program

  • Implement a structured Information Security Management System (ISMS)
  • Protect organizational data, systems, and digital services
  • Prevent misuse or illicit exploitation of business assets
  • Establish effective enterprise security governance
  • Manage cybersecurity risks across complex IT ecosystems
  • Strengthen long-term compliance and due diligence

Risk-Based Cybersecurity Approach

At Clarent360, cybersecurity begins with understanding business risk. The Digital Security Program applies a risk-driven methodology that identifies threats capable of impacting operational continuity, regulatory compliance, and organizational reputation.

  • Security controls are proportional to risk exposure
  • Policies support real operational environments
  • Employees understand their cybersecurity responsibilities
  • Organizations maintain measurable security maturity

Governance, Roles & Security Accountability

Clarent360 establishes defined cybersecurity roles and responsibilities across the organization, ensuring consistent governance and operational security management.

  • Company-wide participation in security initiatives

  • Defined user obligations for handling sensitive data

  • Standardized security procedures and policies

  • Continuous monitoring and improvement
