ISO 22301:2019

Introducing ISO 22301:2019 – Business Continuity Management System

Globally recognized best practice, ISO 22301, provides the robust framework and flexibility you need to prepare for, respond to, and recover from disruptive incidents. It helps you continually review and refine your resilience processes, building business continuity capability today, while ensuring readiness for tomorrow.

The benefits of certification

Independent certification demonstrates your organization’s commitment to resilience and operational excellence.

By gaining third-party assurance that your business continuity management system (BCMS) meets the requirements of ISO 22301, you can inspire confidence in your ability to maintain critical operations during disruption, protect stakeholders, and build trust with an internationally recognized mark of excellence.

Why organizations require ISO 22301?

Organizations adopt ISO 22301:2019 to establish a structured and internationally recognized approach for managing business continuity risks. In today’s interconnected environment, organizations depend heavily on the uninterrupted availability of people, processes, technology, and supply chains. ISO 22301 provides a systematic framework to protect these critical functions while supporting business objectives.

Resilience & Risk Management

The standard enables organizations to identify threats to operations, assess the potential impact of disruptions, prioritize recovery activities, and implement appropriate continuity controls. Rather than responding reactively to crises, organizations build proactive resilience against natural disasters, cyberattacks, supply chain failures, and other operational disruptions.

Regulatory & Compliance

ISO 22301 also supports legal, regulatory, and contractual compliance. Many industries — including financial services, healthcare, utilities, and government — require organizations to demonstrate robust continuity and recovery capabilities. Certification provides verifiable evidence that business continuity practices are properly designed, tested, and maintained, helping organizations meet regulatory obligations and customer expectations.

Trust & Advantage

Another key driver is trust and competitive advantage. Certification reassures customers, partners, and stakeholders that the organization can continue to deliver services even during adverse events. Many clients and government contracts now require demonstrated continuity capability as a prerequisite for engagement, making ISO 22301 certification a strategic enabler for market access and growth.

Organizational Governance

The standard strengthens organizational governance by defining roles, responsibilities, policies, and recovery objectives. Business continuity becomes a management responsibility embedded across the organization rather than solely an IT or facilities function. It also builds confidence among employees, customers, and investors that the organization is prepared to face adversity.

Continuous Improvement

Finally, ISO 22301 promotes a culture of continuous improvement through regular exercises, testing, audits, and corrective actions. This ensures continuity plans remain current, effective, and aligned with evolving threats and organizational changes.

Where is your organization on the path to business continuity maturity?

Certified BCMS Capability

With a mature and certified Business Continuity Management System (BCMS) in place, organizations can maintain the services and functions they are responsible for during disruptions, as well as unlock additional confidence and advantages for the future.

Protect & Mitigate

This includes effectively protecting customers and minimizing the impact of disruptions, to opening doors to new growth opportunities, regardless of industry or region.
